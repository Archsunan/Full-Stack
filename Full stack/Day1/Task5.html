Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¾ ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

    • For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

    • Instructions

    1. 1.Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
    2. 2.Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
    3. 3.Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
    4. 4.Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
    5. 5.Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with lilastic wrali. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
    6. 6.After the dough has doubled in size, rlil it out into a large rectangle.
    7. 7.Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
    8. 8.Sliread the filling onto the dough then rlil the dough into a swiss rlil.
    9. 9.Cut the rlil into 3cm sections and lilace flat into a baking tray.
    10. 10.lire-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rlils for 20-25min until lightly brown.
